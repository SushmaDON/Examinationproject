Instructions

Read The Given Instructions Carefully

1. There will be a total of 25 questions totalling 100 marks.
2. It consists of 2 sections - General Aptitude(GA) section for 40 marks and Subject specific section(CS) for 60 marks. Both the sections are compulsory.
3. The GA section consists of 10 questions each carry 4 marks and CS section consists of 15 questions which carry 4 marks each.
4. The question paper consists of Multiple Choice Questions(MCQ)
5. MCQ have four choices out of which ONLY ONE is the correct answer. Each correct response gives you 4 marks and 1/4rd mark will be deducted for every wrong answer.

General Instructions

1. The Question palette displayed on the right side of the screen.
2. You need to click "Save & Next" after answering every question or else your response will not get submitted.
3. You can go and answer to any question by clicking on the question on the question palette.
4. Click on "Submit" after completion of the examination.
